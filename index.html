Studying Fontys Students' Time Management & Happiness

I designed and conducted a survey for 8 Fontys students to study their time management habits and how these related to their overall happiness. I analyzed the data using Python — primarily with Pandas and Matplotlib — to uncover statistical relationships between study time, stress, and satisfaction levels.

Based on these findings, I created personalized, data-driven recommendations for each student to improve their productivity and happiness. This project demonstrates my ability to collect, clean, analyze, and communicate insights from real-world data.
